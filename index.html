Frequently Asked Questions

▼ What does homelessness mean?

Homelessness means living without a safe or permanent place to stay.

▼ What causes homelessness?

Poverty, unemployment, disasters, and family problems can cause homelessness.

▼ Why is homelessness an important issue?

Homelessness is important because it affects people’s basic needs like shelter, food, and safety, and it is a serious social issue that impacts the whole community.

▼ How does homelessness affect the environment?

Homelessness impacts the environment through increased waste accumulation, pollution of waterways, and damage to natural habitats.

▼ What challenges do homeless people face?

Warmth, dry clothes, water and food are never guaranteed each day.
