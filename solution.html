Building Better Futures Together

Homelessness in Yangon affects thousands of vulnerable families, children, and elderly people. Through education, healthcare, housing, and community support, we can help build safer and more sustainable futures in Myanmar.

Support The Cause

Homelessness In Yangon

Poverty

Rising living costs and unemployment force many families into unsafe living conditions across Yangon.

Natural Disasters

Floods and fires often destroy homes and leave vulnerable communities displaced.

Children At Risk

Homeless children may lose access to education, healthcare, and protection.

Solutions To Homelessness

Communities, charities, and organizations in Yangon can work together to improve lives and reduce homelessness in Myanmar.

Affordable Housing

Safe and affordable housing programs can help low-income families and displaced communities rebuild stable lives in Yangon.

Education Access

Free schools, learning materials, and community education programs can help homeless children continue learning.

Job Opportunities

Skills training and fair employment opportunities can help families become financially independent.

Community Support

Donations, food programs, healthcare, and volunteer activities provide hope and stability for vulnerable people.

Environmental Protection

Clean water systems, waste management, and sustainable housing create healthier living environments in Yangon.

Healthcare Support

Medical care, counseling services, and nutrition programs help improve public health in homeless communities.
